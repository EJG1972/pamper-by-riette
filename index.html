Pamperlang Skoonheid — Please read before using our products

Please read the following information carefully before using our products.

🌿 Natural Skincare Products

  • Our skincare products are made with natural ingredients and botanical extracts.
  • Results may vary from person to person.
  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
  • If irritation or redness occurs, discontinue use and consult a dermatologist.
  • Perform a patch test before first use, especially if you have sensitive skin.
  • Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.

🛁 Bath & Body Care Products

  • Our bath and body products are for external use only.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes.
  • If irritation occurs, discontinue use.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Natural ingredients may cause variations in colour, texture or fragrance. This does not affect product quality.

🌸 Essential Oils

  • Essential oils are highly concentrated and intended for external use only.
  • Do not ingest.
  • Dilute properly before use on skin.
  • Not recommended for use during pregnancy, breastfeeding or on children without professional advice.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Store in a cool, dark place.
  • Consult a healthcare professional if you have a medical condition.

Your safety is important to us. Thank you for choosing Pamperlang Skoonheid. 💗
